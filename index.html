Quick answers to the most common questions about how ReceiptHub works.

What is ReceiptHub exactly?

>
ReceiptHub helps you generate high-quality, 1:1 style receipts tailored for your reselling needs. You choose a template, fill the details, and get a clean receipt ready to send to your buyer or save for your records.

How do I place an order or get access?

>
Just pick a plan in the pricing section, click “Buy now”, and complete checkout via SellAuth. Once payment is confirmed, you’ll receive instant access and instructions on how to use ReceiptHub.

How long does it take after purchase?

>
Access is delivered almost instantly. You’ll normally be set up within a couple of minutes. If anything goes wrong, you can contact support 24/7 on Discord or Telegram.

Can I customize the receipts?

>
Yes. You can customize store name, products, prices, dates, order IDs, addresses and more depending on the template. The goal is to match the style you need while keeping everything clean and consistent.

Do I get help setting everything up?

>
Absolutely. Our team is available 24/7 to help you choose templates, fix any issues and answer questions about best practices for using ReceiptHub in your workflow.
